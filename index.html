About

Researcher. Trainer. Builder.

I am Dr. Aran Castro A J, an Earth Scientist holding a PhD in Applied Geology from Annamalai University. My work sits at the intersection of geospatial data science, hydrological modelling, and environmental hazard assessment.

As Research Coordinator at the Integrated Centre for Water Management (ICWM), Kochi, and GIS Manager at Geospatial Campus, I lead industrial geospatial projects, train the next generation of GIS professionals, and contribute to water management research across India.

From mapping drought vulnerability in Somalia to modelling landslide susceptibility in the Western Ghats, my research traverses scales and geographies — always grounded in rigorous spatial analysis and driven by real-world application.
